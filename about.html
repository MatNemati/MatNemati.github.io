About Me

I am an undergraduate Electrical Engineering student at Isfahan University of Technology. I have a deep passion for interdisciplinary science, particularly the fascinating field of neuroscience.

My academic interests span neuroscience, signal processing, machine learning, deep learning, and the possible intersections between the brain and quantum phenomena. I enjoy solving problems that require creative and algorithmic thinking.

I am also curious about the philosophical and computational aspects of the mind, and I aim to contribute to the growing field of neurotechnology.
